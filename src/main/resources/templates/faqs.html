Frequently Asked Questions

How do I write a review?
To write a review, navigate to any book's detail page and scroll down to the review section. You can rate the book from 1 to 5 stars and write your thoughts about it. Remember, you can only write one review per book.
Can I edit or delete my review?
Yes, you can delete your reviews at any time. Look for the delete button (trash icon) next to your review. Currently, editing reviews is not supported - you'll need to delete your review and write a new one.
How is the book rating calculated?
The book rating is the average of all user ratings. Each user can rate a book from 1 to 5 stars. The overall rating is displayed with one decimal place (e.g., 4.5/5).
What are trending books?
Trending books are determined by a combination of factors including recent ratings, number of reviews, and overall rating. These are typically the most popular books that users are currently engaging with.
Can I submit an anonymous review?
Yes, when writing a review, you can check the "Post Anonymously" option. Your review will be displayed without your username, but you can still manage it from your dashboard.
How do I find specific books?
You can use the search bar at the top of the dashboard to search for books by title, author, or genre. You can also browse books by category in the Library section.
