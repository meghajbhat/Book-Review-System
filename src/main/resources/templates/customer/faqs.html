Frequently Asked Questions

📚 General Questions

Simply create an account, and you'll have access to all our features! You can start by browsing books, writing reviews, connecting with other readers, and participating in our interactive challenges.

🎮 Challenges & Games

The Book Trivia Quiz tests your knowledge about various books and authors. Answer multiple-choice questions correctly to earn points and compete with other readers!

In Detective Cases, you'll solve book-themed mysteries by analyzing clues and making deductions. Each case has multiple clues, and you earn points based on how quickly you solve them.

Book Bingo is a fun reading challenge where you complete different reading tasks to fill your bingo card. Complete rows, columns, or diagonals to win points and unlock achievements!

In Character Match, you'll be presented with scrambled book titles. Unscramble them correctly to earn points. The fewer attempts you need, the more points you earn!

🏆 Points & Achievements

You can earn points through various activities:
  • Writing book reviews
  • Completing challenges
  • Winning games
  • Participating in community activities

Each challenge has its own leaderboard where you can see how you rank against other players. Compete for the top spots to show off your literary expertise!
